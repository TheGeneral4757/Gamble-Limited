# Game Ideas for RNG-THING Casino

## Easy to Implement
- **Scratch Cards** - Buy a card, scratch to reveal prizes (simple random outcomes)
- **High/Low** - Guess if next card is higher or lower (uses existing card system)
- **Dice Roll** - Bet on sum of 2 dice, specific numbers, or ranges
- **Wheel of Fortune** - Spin a wheel with various multipliers

## Medium Difficulty
- **Video Poker** - Hold/discard cards, pay based on poker hand
- **Keno** - Pick numbers, random draw, pay based on matches
- **Baccarat** - Simple banker/player betting
- **Craps** - Multiple bet types, dice-based

## Complex / Future
- **Poker (Multiplayer)** - Texas Hold'em with other users
- **Sports Betting** - Bet on simulated/real events
- **Horse Racing** - Animated races with odds
- **Lottery** - Weekly draws, jackpot accumulation

## Novelty Ideas
- **Rock Paper Scissors** - Quick 50/50 games
- **Number Guess** - Guess 1-100, closer = better payout
- **Trivia Gamble** - Answer questions for multipliers
- **Death Match** - Two users bet, winner takes all

## Seasonal/Event Games
- **Holiday Themed Slots** - Special symbols for events
- **Tournament Mode** - Compete for leaderboard prizes
- **Mystery Box** - Random item/reward drops
